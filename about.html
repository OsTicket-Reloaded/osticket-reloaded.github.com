About osTicket Reloaded

osTicket Reloaded is an Open Source Helpdesk that supports several languages. We are working on an improved version of osTicket that is multilingual and that is extermely user friendly.

Our team

We currently work with two main members, our lead developer Carlos Delfino and Jasper Frumau, our project manager and front end developer. In our translation team we work with several volunteers. We currrently support over 7 languages - English, Spanish, Dutch, Arabic, Brazilian, Swedish, Italian
